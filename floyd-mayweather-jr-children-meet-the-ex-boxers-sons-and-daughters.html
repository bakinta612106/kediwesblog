Meet The Ex-Boxers Sons and Daughters

Floyd Mayweather Jr. is a father of 5 children; 3 sons and 2 daughters. The names of his children are Koraun Mayweather, Iyanna Mayweather, Zion Mayweather, Jirah Mayweather, and his adopted son Devion Cromwell.

Get to know more about Mayweather’s kids here, who gave birth to each, his relationship with them, and what each of his children are up to.

Koraun Mayweather is Floyd Mayweather’s First Child

  • Age: 24 years old
  • Date of Birth: November 7, 1999
  • Mother: Josie Harris
  • Education: West Ranch High School
  • Occupation: Singer, Songwriter
  • Instagram: @kingkoraun

Of all Floyd Mayweather Jr’s children, Koraun Mayweather is the first child of the legendary boxer born on November 7, 1999, in the United States of America. He is currently 24 years old. The name of his mother is Josie Harris, an author, actress, and entrepreneur. She has featured in some movies and series like ‘Exit 38’- 2006, ‘Starter Wives Confidential’- 2013.

At the time of Koraun’s birth, his parents Floyd Mayweather and Josie Harris were in a seemingly happy relationship, as the duo had been dating for about four years before then. Owing to the affluent lifestyle of Floyd Mayweather as a successful professional boxer, it is obvious Koraun enjoyed a comfortable lifestyle, with everything he needed readily available to him.

The celebrity child attended West Ranch High School, California, after which he proceeded to an undisclosed college to earn a degree.

He Had a Rocky Relationship with His Father

Koraun and his father have not always enjoyed a great father-son relationship. The rift in their relationship was caused by Floyd’s alleged physical abuse of Koraun’s mother, Josie Harris, which Koraun had witnessed.

Although the youngster had also publicly called his father a coward, the relationship between the duo is going through a healing phase, with Floyd Mayweather trying his best to win his son’s love and trust.

Koraun Mayweather is Pursuing a Career as a Musician

Koraun, rather than following his father’s career path as a boxer, decided to pursue a career in music. The young man is well on his way to building a successful career as a rapper, singer, and songwriter.

He debuted in music with the launch of his first rap song titled ‘King Koraun- RACK$’ after he graduated from high school. The music video, which was released on May 31, 2018, has gathered 451k views on YouTube and set the pace for Koraun’s sojourn in the music industry. His other released songs are ‘Going Up’ – 2020 and his debut album ‘WHOA’ – 2020

He is Very Active on Social Media

Koraun s very active on various social media platforms. His YouTube channel, which he created on 5 December 2017, currently has 3.11k subscribers. He constantly uploads videos of his music projects on the channel. Additionally, Koraun’s Instagram account has 372k followers who view his updates.

Iyana Mayweather’s Second Child was Born by His Girlfriend Melissa Brim

  • Age: 23 years old
  • Date of Birth: May 20, 2000
  • Mother: Melissa Brim
  • Child: Kentrell Gaulden Jr.
  • Occupation: Model, Rapper
  • Instagram: @moneyyaya

Iyanna is the second daughter of Floyd Mayweather, and the only child he has with his ex-girlfriend Melissa Brim. She was born on May 20, 2000, and is currently 23 years old. Her parents met at the Las Vegas casino and enjoyed an on-and-off relationship.

After her birth, the duo broke up but were jointly involved in the upbringing of their daughter. Iyana’s mother Melissa Brim is a businesswoman who owns ‘Devanna Love Boutique’, named after her two children; Devion and Iyana.

Iyanna Mayweather Has a Son with Rapper NBA YoungBoy

In January 2021, Iyana welcomed a son with her rapper boyfriend, NBA YoungBoy, whom she has been dating since 2019. Their son is named Kentrell Gaulden Jr. and his birth was confirmed by Floyd Mayweather in a 2020 interview with Hollywood Unlocked.

Iyana is Currently Awaiting Sentencing for Stabbing Lapattra Jacobs

In April 2020, Iyanna allegedly stabbed Lapattra Jacobs, NBA YoungBoy’s baby mama during an altercation. The incident happened at the rapper’s home with whom Iyana was in a relationship at the time. Although the details of the stabbing are still unclear, following her arrest, Iyanna alleged it was done merely out of self-defense.

She was charged with aggravated assault and transferred to Houston’s Harris County Jail from where she was released on a bond of $30,000. However, on April 13, 2022, Iyana pleaded guilty to aggravated assault with a deadly weapon, and awaits sentencing.

She Has a Large Social Media Followership

Iyanna is active on Instagram as @Moneyyaya. She has 1.4 million followers and posts beautiful pictures of her life on the page. Her large followership on social media has gained her the status of an influencer, with brands like FashionNova contacting her for modeling gigs.

Zion Mayweather is Floyd Mayweather’s Second Son

  • Age: 22 years old
  • Date of Birth: March 3, 2001
  • Mother: Josie Harris
  • Education: West Ranch High School
  • Occupation: Social Media Personality
  • Instagram: @zion_mayweather

Zion Shamaree Mayweather is the second biological son Floyd Mayweather had with Josie Harris. He was born on March 3, 2001, and is currently 22 years old. At the time he was born, his parents were still dating, but unmarried.

He is a Talented Fashion Designer

Zion founded a clothing brand called ‘Philthy Rich Boys’ for which he designs clothes. He frequently uploads pictures of his latest designs on his Instagram page with 235k followers. His designs include basketball shorts, luxury t-shirts, and other high-end clothing items.

Additionally, Zion also promotes events and uses his social media platform to influence brands and products.

Jirah is Mayweather’s Last and Youngest Child with Josie Harris

  • Age: 20 years old
  • Date of Birth: June 20, 2003
  • Mother: Josie Harris
  • Education: West Ranch High School
  • Occupation: Instagram Star
  • Instagram: @jirahmilan

Jirah is the last child of Floyd and Josie. She was born on June 20, 2003, and is currently 20 years old. On March 10, 2022, when Jirah was 17 years old, her mother was found dead in a car parked outside her home in California. Following an autopsy, the cause of death was established as an accidental overdose. Her death greatly affected Jirah as the two had a very close mother-daughter relationship.

Following her mother’s death, Jirah made a Twitter post on August 10, 2020, accusing Floyd Mayweather of not having his children’s happiness at heart. This confirms the fact that Floyd does not have a close relationship with his daughter.

The Teenager is Yet to Start a Career

At 18, Jirah is yet to chart a career path. However, she is on her way to becoming a social media star as she currently has 274k followers on her Instagram account, where she actively uploads pictures of herself living a good life.

Devion Cromwell is Mayweather’s Adopted Son

  • Age: 27 years old
  • Date of Birth: 1996
  • Mother: Melissa Brim
  • Occupation: Entrepreneur
  • Instagram: @kingxcromwell

Of all Floyd Mayweather Jr children, Devion Cromwell is the eldest. He is the child Melissa Brim had from a previous relationship. He was born in 1999 and is currently 27 years old, although we could not verify details about his exact date of birth, or the identity of his biological father.

When Melissa started dating Floyd, Devion was adopted by the legendary boxer and has been referred to as his adopted son ever since.

His Relationship with Floyd is Tumultuous

Devion once alleged that Floyd was too controlling, and had tried to choke him. This came after Devion referred to Floyd as ‘not his birth father’, resulting in Floyd furiously grabbing him. The incident happened during the Halloween festival in 2014 and led to Devion calling the police, although he filed no lawsuits in pursuance. The duo has not spoken to each other ever since the altercation as reports have it.

He Lives a Glamorous Life

Devion is rumored to be an entrepreneur, although we could not confirm his source of income. Judging by the beautiful pictures he puts up on his Instagram account where he has 20k followers, Devion is living a glamorous life. He has been seen vacationing at luxurious locations like Vancouver, Los Angeles, Mexico, and Jamaica. Amongst his fleet of cars is a Ferrari and a Mercedes 6×6 Brabus G700.

How Many Kids Does Floyd Mayweather have?

Floyd Mayweather has 4 biological kids and one adopted son. His children are sons Koraun Mayweather and Zion Mayweather, and daughters Iyanna Mayweather and Jirah Mayweather. Floyd’s adopted son is Devion Cromwell.

What are Floyd Mayweather’s Kids’ Ages?

Koraun Mayweather is 24 years old while Zion Mayweather is 22 years old. Daughters Iyanna Mayweather is 23 years old while Jirah Mayweather is 20 years old. Floyd’s adopted son, Devion Cromwell, is 27 years old.

Who are the The Mothers of Floyd Mayweather’s Children?

Three of Floyd Mayweather Jr children; Koraun, Zion, and Jirah, were born by Josie Harris. The other two, Iyana and Devon, were born by Melissa Brim.

Did Floyd Mayweather’s Daughter Get Pregnant?

Floyd Mayweather’s daughter Iyana got pregnant with her boyfriend rapper NBA YoungBoy. The lovers welcomed their son Kentrell Gaulden Jr. in January 2021.

Did Floyd Mayweather Disown his Daughter?

Following the arrest of Iyana Mayweather for aggravated assault with a deadly weapon, her father Floyd deleted all photos of her on his Instagram page. Although he did not explicitly disown her, the boxer and his daughter have since mended their relationship.
