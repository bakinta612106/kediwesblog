Matthew McConaughey Has Beard, Camila Alves Goes Blonde: Golden Globes

These two! Matthew McConaughey and his wife Camila Alves had eyes only for each other as they walked the Golden Globes red carpet in Beverly Hills on Sunday, Jan. 11. The Oscar winner, in particular, looked handsome with slicked back hair and a scruffy beard.

McConaughey, 45, is nominated for Best Actor in a Mini-Series or Motion Picture Made for Television for his work in HBO's True Detective, going head-to-head with his costar Woody Harrelson. Last year, he won a Golden Globe and an Oscar for his stellar performance in Dallas Buyers Club.

Alves, for her part, wore a blush Monique Lhuillier column dress with a ruffled top on the red carpet. She also debuted blonder locks in an updo. She first teased her new hair color via Twitter on Jan. 8. "Never been blonde before. Working up courage for a change. Should I do it?" she tweeted at the time. "Have appointment tomorrow! Thoughts? Help!" By Saturday night, she had taken the plunge!
