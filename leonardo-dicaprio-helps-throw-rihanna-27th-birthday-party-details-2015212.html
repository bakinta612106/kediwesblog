Leonardo DiCaprio Helps Throw Rihanna 27th Birthday Party: Details!

A good time for RiRi!

Leonardo DiCaprio helped throw Rihanna a star-studded affair for her 27th birthday on Friday, Feb. 20, an insider exclusively reveals to Us Weekly. The shindig took place at a private home in Beverly Hills and went past 4 a.m. in the morning.

"There were balloons that spelled out her name set up," the source tells Us. The "FourFiveSeconds" singer dressed in a plunging dress and kept her dark locks down and slicked back.

Rihanna also was surprised with a leopard print three-tier cake. "Naomi Campbell was right next to her when she blew out the candles," the source says. Other famous faces in attendance included Mick Jagger, Beyonce, Jay Z, Russell Simmons, model Angela Martini, Tommy Chiabra, and Richie Akiva.

As exclusively reported, Rihanna and DiCaprio, 40, recently spent Valentine's Day together. They've been meeting up for months, and continued the fun on her birthday. The insider adds: "They were dancing close all night."
