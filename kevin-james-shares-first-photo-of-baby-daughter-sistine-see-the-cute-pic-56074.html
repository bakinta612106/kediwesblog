Kevin James Shares First Photo of Baby Daughter Sistine See the Cute Pic!

Introducing, Baby Sistine!

During a new interview on ‘Live with Kelly and Michael,’ Kevin James shared the first photo of his newborn daughter, Sistine — and the tiny tot could not be cuter!

After debuting the snapshot, the father-of-four joked his three-month-old is already gearing up to enter the acting world just like her famous dad.

Kevin’s youngest daughter, Sistine.

“She’s talented, too, I got to be honest. I got her working on a Robert De Niro impression,” Kevin, 49, told hosts Kelly Ripa and Michael Strahan.

Related: Jenna Bush Hager is Pregnant with Baby No. 2

The ‘Paul Blart: Mall Cop 2’ star and his wife, actress Steffiana de la Cruz, 40, welcomed Sistine in January. The couple — who married in 2004 — are already parents to daughters Sienna-Marie, 9, and Shea, 7, and a son, Kannon, nearly 4.

Kevin and his wife, Steffiana, in July 2013.

As his Hollywood career continues to skyrocket, Kevin confessed he doesn’t like to be away from his family — so they hit the road with him!

“They travel everywhere with me,” he said of his children during the Monday, April 13 sit-down. “[Sistine]’s the best little traveler. She doesn’t cry — only like her father, she cries when she wants to eat and that’s about it.”
