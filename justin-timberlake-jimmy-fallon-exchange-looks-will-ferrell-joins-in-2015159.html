Justin Timberlake, Jimmy Fallon Exchange Looks; Will Ferrell Joins In

They clearly get each other. Justin Timberlake and Jimmy Fallon's bromance reached an even deeper level on The Tonight Show on Monday, Sept. 14, when a video piece of the two old friends revealed that they don't need verbal communication to understand each other.

In fact, the stars exchange a minute and 30 seconds worth of "Looks" in the clip, which include knowing glances, goofy secret smiles, downcast nods of understanding, and more.

The cameras then pan out and show the pair huddled together in a dressing room. Neither star still says a word, then suddenly… Will Ferrell makes a surprise cameo appearance!

Both Timberlake, 34, and Fallon, 40, teased the bit on Monday via Twitter.

Their latest bit was revealed after the dynamic duo dominated headlines last week for several reasons. Timberlake stopped by The Tonight Show and performed the History of Rap 6 with his pal. Timberlake was asked by Fallon to judge a very special-edition Lip Sync Battle featuring Ellen DeGeneres.
