Becki Newton News - Us Weekly

Name:Becki Newton

Hometown:Guilford, CT

Birthday:July 4, 1978

About

Born in 1978, Becki Newton appeared in musicals and plays at local theaters growing up in Guilford, Conn. Before landing her gig on the ABC’s hit comedy Ugly Betty, she had a hosting stint on TBS’s Movie Junky and had memorable guest roles on Cold Case, Law & Order: SVU and Charmed. However, Newton’s portrayal of office diva Amanda Tanen on Ugly Betty catapulted her to star status.

The actress has been married to actor Chris Diamantopoulos since May 2005. In November 2010, the couple welcomed their first son.
