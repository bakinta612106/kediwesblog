Spencer Pratt News - Us Weekly

Name:Spencer Pratt

Hometown:Los Angeles, California

Birthday:August 14, 1983

About

Spencer Pratt starred on the MTV reality series The Hills from 2007 to 2010. He was dating costar Heidi Montag at the time, and the twosome married in 2008. They welcomed sons Gunner and Ryker in 2017 and 2022, respectively.

Since his time on The Hills, Pratt has also appeared on reality shows I’m a Celebrity…Get Me Out of Here! and Celebrity Big Brother. He rejoined The Hills cast for a two-season spinoff from 2019 to 2021, The Hills: New Beginnings. Pratt also runs a crystal company, Pratt Daddy Crystals, with Montag.
