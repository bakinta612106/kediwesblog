Download Smart Driver Care for Windows - Free

Smart Driver Care is a program that revises your system and fetches any necessary files for your PC. It also automates the update process.

Automatic scans

Smart Driver Care sifts through your computer’s drivers to check for any updates. The intuitive software will also review and pick up any corrupt files that need to be deleted.

Individuals and businesses alike depend on their computers to function at an optimum level. Smart Driver Care ensures your PC’s drivers are up to date automatically, decreasing the hassle and saving you time. It also picks up malfunctioning drivers and gives you the option to either delete them or fix them.

You can schedule scans when it’s most convenient for you. It also deletes old drivers that you no longer need to have on your computer.

During a scan, the intuitive program scans your computers files and drivers and provides a list of all the updates you need execute. At times, though, the software might skip certain files. A simple reboot can fix this issue. It’s worth noting that the software doesn’t download any strange add-ons or malware.

The application retrieves information and updates from recognized, original equipment manufacturers every time. There are no third-party sources for any downloaded data.

You can decide when you want to run scans on your PC. There’s an option to schedule different actions, as well. You can also stop any scan if needed.

The platform features a huge database of drivers and boasts a very effective search engine.

Where can you run this program?

Smart Driver Care can run on Windows 10, 8.1, 8 and 7.

Is there a better alternative?

No. Smart Driver Care is easy to use and functions properly. It’s a simple and effective way to keep your drivers up-to-date. Alternatives, like Driver Restore, have limited options and a more frustrating interface.

Our take

Smart Driver Care has the right tools to tackle system updates and corrupted files. It has a comfortable setup and simplifies the entire update process. Where the application falls short, however, is when it skips certain files.

Should you download it?

Yes. If you’re looking for a program to keep your computer up-to-date without the hassle of searching for files yourself, this is a fine choice.
