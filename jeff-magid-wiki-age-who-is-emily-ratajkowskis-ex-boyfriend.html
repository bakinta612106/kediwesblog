Educational background

Career of a music producer, singer and musician

Jeff started his music career in his early years – becoming a professional musician was one of his biggest dreams. It came true when he created his music band “Magid”, and started recording songs and touring around the US.

He is also a popular music producer who has collaborated with many performers, such as Chicago, Gregg Allman, Dinosaur Jr., Quiet Riot, etc. Jeff’s most popular songs were written during the time of his relationship with Emily Ratajkowski, and Jess confirmed that such songs as “Birth Control”, “The Ritual” and “Make It” were inspired by her and their relationship. As of 2019, he was featured in several songs by such performers as Marcy Mane, Zab, K.A.N.T.A., and many others. His music career underwent a popularity drop after his split from Emily Ratajkowski, since a lot of his listeners were primarily her fans, and supported his music just because he was her boyfriend.

R.B.I. Productions

Jeff also owns the company named RBI Productions, which he founded in 1999. Before that, he worked with other production studios and companies, such as Capitol Records in which he worked for more than nine years, and Geffen Records, with which he collaborated for more than 10 years. He also worked with such musicians as Joe Cocker, Duran Duran, Tina Turner, and Electric Light Orchestra, while serving as a producer for those companies. He is a voting member of the Recording Academy, a BMI affiliated songwriter, and an experienced specialist in everything related to mastering music compilations, licensing, marketing in the entertainment sphere, etc.
