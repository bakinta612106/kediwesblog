Gwen Stefani Is Not In A Rush To Marry Blake Shelton And Gavin Rossdale Has Something To Do With It

Is marriage still in the cards for Gwen Stefani? It looks like the answer might be no and Blake Shelton is not at all bothered by this development.

Au contraire, the country star is pretty happy with the situation and has no plans to change or shake what is not broken.

In a recent interview, Gwen confessed that going through the divorce from Gavin Rossdale was very difficult and added: “Going through all these hard times, [you] get to a place where you find somebody that’s like your best friend, that you know you can depend on and trust and go through life – we’re just trying to be in the moment as much as we can.”

A source told Hollywood Life that Gwen is afraid to walk down the aisle a second time and went on to say: “Gwen is hesitant to get married again, but it’s something she deeply wants to do as does Blake when the time feels right. Gwen is afraid to get married because she was so hurt by the ending of her marriage to Gavin Rossdale.”

According to the family friend, the mother of three — (Kingston, 12, Zuma, 10, and Apollo, four) — does not feel confident enough that her marriage will work a second time.

The person revealed: “Though she knows things are different with Blake, she is scared by the entire divorce process to this day. It was really hard for her. Gwen is proud of how far she has come and knows things would be different this time around, but she’s not quite there yet.”

The insider went on to add: “Right now, she’s truly enjoying where things are at with Blake and feels no pressure to wed anytime soon, and Blake isn’t going to push her, either.”
