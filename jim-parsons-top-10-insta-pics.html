Jim Parsons: Top 10 Insta Pics

The ‘Bazinga’ Man in The Big Bang Theory, Jim Parsons is very well known for his craziness in the sitcom. His application of scientific rules in maintaining common human relationships often prove to be quite awkward and irritating. But besides his lectures on String Theory, Jim Parsons and his character Sheldon Cooper , both have been widely appreciated by his fans. He not only has a wide fan following due to his on-screen presence, but also has a wide range of audience appreciating his photographs online. He is an active Instagram user and has around two and a half million followers. His photographs are much liked by his audiences and commented upon. Here is a countdown of his ten most liked photographs on Instagram.
