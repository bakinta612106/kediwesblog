I Am the Fated Villain Chapter 83: Release Date, Recap &amp; Where to Read?

Fans of the popular manhwa series, “I Am the Fated Villain,” are eagerly anticipating the release of chapter 83. In the previous chapter, we witnessed an all-out war between the Gu clan and the Feathered clan. Gu Changge, the fated villain, killed the young master of the Feathered clan, which enraged the clan and resulted in a fierce battle. The elders of the Feathered clan were no match for Gu Changge, but an awakened ancestor of the Feathered clan arrived on the scene to challenge him. Here’s everything you need to know about the release of chapter 83.

Release Date and Time

Chapter 83 of “I Am the Fated Villain” is scheduled to be released on Saturday, May 13th, 2023, at midnight Korean Standard Time. The release date and time in other popular time zones are as follows:

  • Pacific Daylight Time [West Coast]: 7:00 AM PDT (May 12th, 2023)
  • Central Daylight Time [Central American and Canada]: 9:00 AM CDT (May 12th, 2023)
  • Eastern Daylight Time [East Coast]: 10:00 AM EDT (May 12th, 2023)
  • Greenwich Meditarian Time [Britain]: 5:00 PM BST (May 12th, 2023)
  • Indian Standard Time [India]: 8.30 PM IST (May 12th, 2023)
  • Singapore Standard Time [Singapore]: 11:00 PM SGT (May 12th, 2023)
  • Philippines Standard Time [Philippines]: 11:00 PM PST (May 12th, 2023)
  • Korean Standard Time [Korea]: Midnight KST (May 13th, 2023)
  • Japanese Standard Time [Korea]: Midnight JST (May 13th, 2023)
  • Australia Eastern Daylight Time [Australia]: 02:00 AM AEDT (May 13th, 2023)

Where to Read Chapter 83

Fans can read chapter 83 of “I Am the Fated Villain” on Kakao after it is released by the publisher. Additionally, readers can support the creators by purchasing the new volumes of the series.

Recap of Chapter 82

In chapter 82, Gu Changge killed the young master of the Feathered clan and threw his body at the clan, enraging them. He easily defeated the soldiers of the Feathered clan who charged at him, and one of the soldiers who attempted a special move was also defeated. Gu Changge slapped one of the elders of the Feathered clan, and an awakened ancestor of the Feathered clan arrived to challenge him.

Final Thoughts

“I Am the Fated Villain” manhwa fans can look forward to chapter 83, which promises to be full of action and suspense. The release date and time have been announced, and readers can find the chapter on Kakao once it’s available.
