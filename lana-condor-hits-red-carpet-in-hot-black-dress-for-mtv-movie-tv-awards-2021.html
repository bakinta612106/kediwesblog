Lana Condor Hits Red Carpet In Hot Black Dress For MTV Movie &amp; TV Awards 2021

Lana Condor stuns in a gorgeous black dress at the 2021 MTV Movie & TV Awards on Sunday night (May 16) at the Hollywood Palladium in Los Angeles.

The To All The Boys I’ve Loved Before star was joined by fellow Netflix stars, The Kissing Booth‘s Jacob Elordi, and On My Block‘s Sierra Capri, for the annual award show.

Lana‘s movie, To All The Boys: Always and Forever, is nominated for Best Movie, and Best Musical Moment for “Beginning, Middle, End”.

Jacob‘s The Kissing Booth 2 is also up for Best Musical Moment for “Lost in the Wild”.

FYI: Lana wore Giorgio Armani for the award ceremony.

The 2021 MTV Movie & TV Awards are airing RIGHT NOW on MTV! Tune in to see all the winning moments!
