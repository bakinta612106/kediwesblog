Justin Bieber and Diddy Dance-Off at Usher's Surprise Birthday Party

Nothing like a good ole’ dance off to kick off a birthday party! Justin Bieber and Diddy went toe-to-toe in a dance off during Usher’s surprise star-studded birthday bash at Bootsy Bellows on Monday, Oct. 14. The “Yeah” singer celebrated turning 35 alongside some of his famous pals at the West Hollywood club including Blake Shelton, T.I. and Jermaine Dupri, a source reveals to Us Weekly. The appearance by his former Voice pal Shelton “was a huge hit.” Miguel and Sugar Ray Leonard were also in attendance.

“Usher was completely surprised. Guests arrived at 10:30 p.m. and Usher came at 11,” a source told Us. “Bieber came with Scooter [Braun] and hung out with Usher in a booth. Usher’s girlfriend Grace [Miguel] was there as well.”
