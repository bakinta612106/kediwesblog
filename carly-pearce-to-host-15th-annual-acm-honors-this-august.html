Carly Pearce to Host 15th Annual ACM Honors this August Celeb Secrets Country

Carly Pearce is headed to the ACM Honors this summer!

The reigning ACM Female Artist of the Year and four-time ACM Award-winner will return for a second year to host the 15th Annual Academy of Country Music Honors, set to take place on Wednesday, August 24th at The Ryman Auditorium in Nashville.

“ACM Honors is one of my most favorite events of the year,” said Pearce. “It is a privilege to once again host this special night and celebrate those who are making a profound impact on Country Music.”

The unforgettable evening of live music and tributes will feature appearances from multiple honorees, including Miranda Lambert, Connie Bradley, Paul Barnabee, Dwight Wiles, Morgan Wallen, Duane Clark, Chris Stapleton, Sonny Throckmorton, Shania Twain, the cast of Yellowstone, and HARDY. A full lineup of tribute performers and special guests will be announced at a later date.

Fans can see all of the action live and in-person this year, as tickets will be made available to the general public. Beginning, this Thursday (June 23), A-list subscribers and Academy members get first dibs on tickets, with general onsale beginning this Friday (June 24) through AXS.

The event follows ACM Party for a Cause at Ascend Amphitheater the night before, that features performances from artists like Brothers Osborne, Sara Evans, Darius Rucker, Caylee Hammack and Blanco Brown. You can learn more about the full lineup and where to purchase tickets here.
