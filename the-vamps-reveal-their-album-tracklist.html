The Vamps Reveal Their Album Tracklist

The Vamps have revealed the tracklist for the Day edition of their upcoming album Night & Day, due to be available on 20th July 2018. Last year, Brad, James, Connor and Tristan released the Night edition of their album which features hit singles All Night with Matoma and Middle of the Night with Martin Jensen. The album also features other amazing collaborations like Hands with Sabrina Carpenter and Mike Perry and It’s A Lie with TNI. We can’t wait to hear the collaborations on their new album!

As with previous albums, the guys each have their own individual copy of the album available to pre-order which will be signed and have exclusive tracks as Connor has revealed:

Waheeeeey! The pre-orders are up! You can get our own individual versions too, mine might have a pop punk song or two on there ? https://t.co/NkrvJENwoU

— Conor Bell (@TheVampsCon) April 13, 2018

The Day Edition of the album is comprised of a CD and a DVD, giving fans an exclusive insight into the O2 arena show with the live content on the DVD. The tracklist of the CD is as follows:

  • Just My Type
  • Hair Too Long
  • Talk Later
  • Too Good To Be True – Danny Avila & The Vamps feat Machine Gun Kelly
  • For You
  • What Your Father Says
  • Cheap Wine – Vamps & Kriss Kross Amsterdam
  • Personal featuring Maggie Lindemann
  • Time Is Not On Our Side
  • Picture of Us
  • Middle Of The Night
  • All Night
  • Hands
  • Same To You
  • Paper Hearts
  • Shades On
  • It’s a Lie
  • Stay

    • You can preorder The Vamps’ new album as well as album bundles here!

    We are so excited for The Vamps to embark on their Night and Day Tour tomorrow and hopefully they’ll be performing some of these new songs as well as old favourites like Can We Dance and Wild Heart. The guys will be joined on tour by Jacob Sartorius, New Hope Club, HRVY and Maggie Lindemann and their London and Manchester shows will also feature special guest Conor Maynard. Make sure to get your tickets because this is a tour you certainly don’t want to miss!
