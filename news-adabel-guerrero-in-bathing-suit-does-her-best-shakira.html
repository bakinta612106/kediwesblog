Adabel Guerrero in Bathing Suit Does Her Best &quot;Shakira&quot; Celebwell

Adabel Guerrero shares a lot of gorgeous photos and videos on her Instagram. Guerrero channeled Shakira in a recent video she posted. In it, Guerrero danced by the pool. She wore a strapless pink leopard print bathing suit, showing off her figure. She captioned the post, "When you take your braids out and you look like Shakira!" How does she stay so fit? Read on to see 5 ways Adabel Guerrero stays in shape and the photos that prove they work—and to get beach-ready yourself, don't miss these essential 30 Best-Ever Celebrity Bathing Suit Photos!

Guerrero posts a lot of workout videos on Instagram, giving her followers a look at what she does to stay in shape. One kind of exercise that she does a lot of are leg and lower body workouts. She shared this video of some of her favorite lower body exercises. In it, Guerrero is seen doing squats on releve, side squat walks, and lunges. She captioned the post, "Legs are one of the hardest muscles to build, but with these tips you can give them strength and tone, from home and WITHOUT materials."

Guerrero believes that you don't have to go to the gym to exercise. A lot of her workout videos are done at home without equipment. She shared some at-home exercises and tips in this video. In it, Guerrero is seen doing dips and bicycle crunches with a chair, and squats with leg lifts. She captioned the post, "To train there are no excuses!!! In this routine you only need a chair, water, and a desire to boost your physical and mental health."

Guerrero might spend a lot of time outside, but that doesn't mean she thinks it's ideal for working out. She advised against this in the caption of this Instagram post. "I don't recommend training in the sun. It's better when it's cooler since it's getting really hot. Drink lots of water!!!"

One thing Guerrero does to stay in shape is practice yoga. While on a trip to Playa del Carmen, she made sure to do some poses on the beach. In the first photo of this series she posted on Instagram, Guerrero is seen doing a handstand against a tree. She captioned it, "With you until the end."

Guerrero loves to swim as a way of staying in shape. She spends a lot of time in the water and shares photos of herself doing so on Instagram. In the first photo of this series, Guerrero and her daughter are seen in the pool together. Also, in this vacation reel, Guerrero is seen going for a swim in her hotel's pool.
