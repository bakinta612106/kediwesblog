Mike Tyson Returns Evander Holyfields Ear In The Funniest Foot Locker Ad Ever

Foot Locker  just released  one of the funniest commercials of the year starring retired boxers Mike Tyson and Evander Holyfield, retired NBA and NFL superstars Dennis Rodman and Brat Favre, as well as Cleveland Cavaliers (basketball) guard Kyrie Irving and sports reporter Craig Sager.

The ad starts with Irving looking ahead to Foot Locker’s “Week of Greatness” by imagining what might happen when all is right in the world.  All is right includes Tyson returning the chunk he bit off of Holyfields’s ear, which he preserved in formaldehyd.

The hilarious clips also shows Rodman at a airport buying a one way ticket to North Korea and people cheering in the background, “He’s never coming back!”

In addition,  Sager burns his loud tacky suits he’s famous for, saying, “Good bye old friends,” as he throws one into the flames.

This commercial doesn’t say much about buying sneakers, but it is a masterpiece. Check it out in the video player above.
