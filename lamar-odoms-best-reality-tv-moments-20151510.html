Lamar Odom's Best Reality TV Moments

Remember that one time Khloe Kardashian and Rob Kardashian tried to teach Lamar Odom how to swim? The classic Khloe & Lamar moment was just one of the many scenes that made us laugh and fall in love with the former NBA star pro, who is currently in critical condition at Sunrise Hospital in Las Vegas after being found unconscious at brothel Love Ranch South on Oct. 13.

During one of his first appearances on the family’s hit reality show, Keeping Up With the Kardashians, Odom professed his love to Khloe after just a month of dating in 2009. Relive some of the 35-year-old’s other top TV moments below.

1. When Lamar Proposed to Khloe:

“I want to hang out forever,” he told Khloe in 2009. “I want to get married right now. We met on the 27th. Your birthday is on the 27th. So we should get married on the 27th…do you remember the last flight I took from New York? [It] was United Flight 27.”

2. His Fear of the Dentist:

“I’m afraid of the dentist because most likely I’m gonna have to get a lot of work done,” the candy lover revealed on KUWTK, prompting his then-sister-in-law Kim Kardashian to quip, “It’s so funny to see such a big man squirm over the word dentist.”

3. When He Questioned Khloe’s Bathroom Habits:

“Do you use the bathroom?” the 6-foot-10 former Lakers player jokingly teased his then-wife in 2011, hinting that she only went when he left the house.

4. Learning to Swim:

“I’m so excited that Lamar actually agreed to let me teach him how to swim,” Khloe told the cameras in 2010 before hilarity ensued while Rob Kardashian tried to help Odom float.
