Emily Maynard Shares Cute Photo of Baby Boy and Daughter Ricki

Love at first sight! The Bachelorette alum Emily Maynard shared a precious photo of her two children — baby boy Jennings Tyler Johnson and daughter Ricki, 10 — on Instagram on Thursday, July 23.

“Best buddies already,” the proud mom captioned the shot. “Y’all my heart is going to explode.”

PHOTOS: Cutest baby announcements

In the photo, Jennings (whom the family affectionately call JT) and older sister Ricki are cuddled up together on what looks like a bed, the tiny baby boy nestled in a green-and-white polka dot blanket.

JT is the first child for Maynard, 28, and husband Tyler Johnson, whom they welcomed last week, sharing a first photo on Thursday, July 16.

The Season 8 Bachelorette exclusively revealed to Us Weekly this past January that she and Johnson were expecting a tiny bundle of joy, and she later revealed in May that Ricki would be getting a baby brother.

“I’m just really looking forward to getting up with him and feeding him,” she told WCCB Charlotte at the time. “Ask me after he’s been here for a month if I’m still excited, but right now, that’s what I’m looking forward to.”
