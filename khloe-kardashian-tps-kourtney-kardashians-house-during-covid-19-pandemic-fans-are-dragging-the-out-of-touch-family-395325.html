Khloe Kardashian TPs Kourtney Kardashians House During COVID-19 Pandemic Fans Are Dragging

Another day, another tone-deaf post from a celebrity during the COVID-19 pandemic. This time Khloe Kardashian used an item that’s hard to find in a prank against her sister.

The scarcity of toilet paper was once joked about before it became a serious need. As stores put limits on the amount you can purchase and people price gauge online — Khloe used toilet paper to TP her sister’s house.

Kourtney Kardashian took to Instagram to show the lawn of her mansion covered in the coveted necessity.

The Neighborhood Talk reposted the clip that was shared to Kourtney’s Instagram story.

Kourtney could be heard saying: ‘So I haven’t had this much excitement in months. This is what Mason and KoKo do when they have a sleepover. It’s actually genius. P, look at this!’

Her daughter Penelope chimed in to say: ‘I know I saw. That was Mason and KoKo, I saw them.’

Kourtney then said that they would do it next time.

Followers understandably slammed the sisters in the comment section.

‘As much toilet tissue I need during this quarantine. I should beat their a**!!’ wrote another.
