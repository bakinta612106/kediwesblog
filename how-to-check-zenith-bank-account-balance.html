Code to Check Zenith Bank Account  Balance?

The Zenith Bank USSD code for checking the account balance is *966*00#. The Zenith bank USSD code works on all Nigerian Network service providers (MTN, Glo, Airtel, and 9mobile). The Zenith Bank USSD code can be used on any mobile device, and it is straightforward to use.

Use the following steps to check your Zenith Bank account via USSD;

  • Dial *966*00# on the phone number linked to your Zenith bank account.
  • Enter your Zenith account number and submit.
  • On the next screen, enter your 4 digit PIN and send.

After entering the PIN successfully, your account balance will be sent to you via SMS, but this service isn’t free. An N15 service fee applies and will be immediately deducted from your account.
