Stranger Things Season 2 Teaser Released

Just in time for Halloween, Netflix will be releasing all new episodes of the hit series ‘Stranger Things’.

The official premiere date for the second season has now been announced for October 27th, which was confirmed recently on Netflix’s Twitter page, in the form of an extremely intriguing poster, giving us a look into what’s going to be going on in Hawkins, Indiana.

Some doors can't be closed. @Stranger_Things season 2. October 27. pic.twitter.com/8Ewfyy4lcA

— Netflix (@netflix) July 11, 2017

The trailer we’ve seen so far shows that season two will be set in 1984 and also gives us our first glimpse of the new big baddie, which looks monstrous and even more threatening than the first we encountered.

How are four kids going to be able to defeat a trans-dimensional monster which practically fills the sky? This is where their strong friendship and help from Eleven will become a priority. We believe in them!

“It’s 1984 and the citizens of Hawkins, Indiana are still reeling from the horrors of the demagorgon and the secrets of Hawkins Lab. Will Byers has been rescued from the Upside Down but a bigger, sinister entity still threatens those who survived.”
