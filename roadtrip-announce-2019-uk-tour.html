RoadTrip announce 2019 UK Tour

Following a successful short run of dates towards the back end of 2019, boyband RoadTrip are heading across the UK again.

The band will hit the road on February 17th at Manchester Academy and will continue on to the likes of Dublin, London and Newcastle. Check out the full list of dates below!

17.02.19 – Manchester, Club Academy

24.02.19 – Glasgow, Classic Grand

09.03.19 – Dublin, Academy

10.03.19 – Birmingham, O2 Academy

31.03.19 – London, O2 Academy Islington

06.04.19 – Sheffield, Corporation

07.04.19 – Newcastle, Riverside

RoadTrip released hit single ‘Take This Home’ in 2018 and travelled Europe as the support act for rising star HRVY. The group have also had great success on YouTube thanks to their boyband-take on some of the biggest hits around and their playful vlogs. The group’s cover of “Galway Girl” being the biggest cover success to date, with over seven million YouTube views.

To catch Rye, Brooklyn, Andy, Jack and Mikey on tour in a city near you, you can snap up your tickets from Wednesday 23rd January.
