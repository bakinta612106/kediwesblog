Star Wars Day deals include apps, games and more, credits will do fine

Another Star Wars Day, another round of discounts on Star Wars merchandise.

This year's deals cross the boundaries between console, mobile and PC games. And they don't stop there, with discounts available on movies, TV shows and Star Wars fiction. Below, you'll find links and overviews of the deals from several retailers.

AMAZON

Amazon's dedicated Star Wars Day page showcases discounts on everything from stormtrooper t-shirts to Blu-Rays and games in the Amazon App Store.

GOG

Previously announced deals include the $38.93 Blaster Bundle with games like X-Wing vs. Tie Fighter and Star Wars Rebellion, as well as the $22.44 Saber Bundle with games like Star Wars: Dark Forces and Star Wars Battlefront 2.

GOOGLE PLAY

Google's Star Wars Day page corrals Android apps and games, as well as movies, music and TV discounts, including Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic for $2.99.

iOS

Discounted Star Wars apps, games and in-app purchases for iPad and iPhone include Star Wars Scene Maker, Star Wars Pinball and Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic.

STEAM

The sale ends May 6, and offers up to 77 percent off on games like the $23 Star Wars collection, which includes 14 games that would normally cost $99.99 and the $26.99 X-Wing bundle with four games that would normally cost $29.99.

PLAYSTATION

Discounts are available on PlayStation 3 games like the Star Wars: The Force Unleashed series and even PlayStation 2 games like Star Wars: Episode 3—Revenge of the Sith. PS3 and PS4 owners can also download a free Millennium Falcon theme.

XBOX

Downloadable deals are available for games in the Lego and the Star Wars: The Force Unleashed series, each for less than $10.
