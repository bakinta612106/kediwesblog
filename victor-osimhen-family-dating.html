Victor is 6 feet 1 inch (1.86 m) tall in height with a proper body weight. 

Professional Career & More

The 23 year old started his career at the Ultimate Strikers Academy based in Lagos, Nigeria. Later he joined Vfl Wolfsburg in early January 2017 signing a three and half year deal with the team.

He then went to Ligue 1 team, Lille in July 2019 per wiki. A year later, a giant Serie A team, Napoli signed him on a club record fee of 70 million Euros on 31 July 2020. It has been two wonderful seasons of Victor with the team and there are also many speculations and rumors of the Nigerian International joining English supergiant, Manchester United in the January 2023 transfer window.
