Alexander James Rodriguez Returns With Steamy Summer Song Verano Para Siempre

One of the most influential Gen Z recording artists on the rise is British pop artist Alexander James Rodriguez. 

Alexander’s latest offering, ‘Verano Para Siempre’ of which he serenades us in perfect Spanish, is filled with soothing marimbas and romantic Latin lyrics, that takes listeners on a sizzling hot tropical journey of salty waves, and sandy toes.

Staying authentic to the summer song, Alexander was recently spotted in Marbella, Spain shooting the cover art at the luxurious Hotel Puente Romano, a favorite celebrity hangout. 

“I am incredibly proud of my Latin heritage and loved creating and recording ‘Verano Para Siempre,’ it is one of many Spanish songs I plan to record.”

“Although I now live in Los Angeles, Marbella will always be considered home, so I couldn’t have selected a better location to shoot the cover art. The chilled and colorful surroundings of the beach at Hotel Puente Romano matches the vibe of the song perfectly.”

Alexander James Rodriguez on ‘Verano Para Siempre’

Written by Grammy®-nominated Dominican singer and songwriter Alih Jey, Alexander James Rodriguez, and Liz Rodriguez, ‘Verano Para Siempre’ which means Summer Forever, showcases the versatility of the teens vocal range and ability to perfectly perform in multiple languages. 

“El verano no se va a acabar
Es para siempre
Y la fiesta no puede parar
Es para siempre
Ven conmigo
Vamos a bailar
Con toda de mi gente
El verano no se va a acabar
Es para siempre”

Alexander James Rodriguez, who turned 15 in June, has already scooped up the Hollywood Music in Media Rising Star Award for his debut single ‘My Crew.’ At the top of 2022, the unsigned teen joined forces with a multi-platinum selling team to take him next level in his music career. 

“I’d love to sign with a Record Label, but in the meantime, I’m going to continue to release original pop music in both English and Spanish that I hope the world can relate to.”

Now in his third year of writing and recording music, the award-winning teen is literally just getting started. ‘Verano Para Siempre’ is definitely this year’s must hear summer smash!
