Jared Leto Unveils Platinum Blonde Hair at Paris Fashion Week: Photos

Sorry, Kim—someone’s stolen your thunder!

Kim Kardashian may have debuted platinum blonde locks at the Balmain fashion show in Paris on Thursday, March 5, but she wasn’t the only one to prove her blonde ambition that day. Just 72 hours after revealing that he chopped his long, ombre locks, Jared Leto debuted bright, white-blonde hair, too—and at the same exact event!

While the Oscar-winning actor, 43, did look stylish in his blue velvet blazer and white turtleneck, teamed with a practical pair of snow boots, we suspect that the dye job was not, in fact, a style statement, but the first in a series of colorings for his role as The Joker in the upcoming 2016 film, Suicide Squad. The natural brunette would have to bleach those locks first in order to achieve the villain’s famously green-tinged hair.

Not that Leto—who’s rocked blonde hair before, most notably during his Fight Club days—is letting on one way or the other. The My So-Called Life alum shared a car selfie en route to the event via Instagram, simply captioned, “#.”
