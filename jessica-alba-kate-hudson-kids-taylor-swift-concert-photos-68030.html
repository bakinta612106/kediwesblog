LOL!Jessica Alba Meets a Fan With Her Face Tattooed on His Arm

Talk about a meta moment!

Jessica Alba recently ran into a fan on the street — and the police officer showed the 35-year-old star that he has her face tattooed on his arm. The best part? The actress documented the funny meet-and-greet on Instagram!

“Definitely one of the more surreal moments of my life. This sweetie pie young officer drove by outside my friend’s house and stopped to tell me he had my face tattooed on his arm — of course I HAD to see it! So cool and such a trip,” Jessica captioned a video shared on the social media outlet.

“Wow, so cool!” one of Jess’s 9.7 million followers commented on the clip with another adding, “That’s crazy but @JessicaAlba is worth it!”

The Good Luck Chuck star has a few tattoos of her own. Earlier this year, the mom-of-two — she shares daughters Honor, 8, and Haven, 5, with husband Cash Warren — revealed the interesting meaning behind her wrist tattoo.
