WATCH: Selena Gomez's 'Same Old Love' video

Last night saw the beautiful and talented Selena Gomez drop her new music video for ‘Same Old Love’.

The video for the follow up single to ‘Good For You’ is directed by Michael Haussman and shows Selena looking stunning in a movie like clip.

The video follows Gomez while she is being driven in a car, and see’s her looking out the windows at rowing and loved up couples. Then we see Selena performing ‘Same Old Love’ on stage at her ‘Revival’ album fan launch party.

Selena is looking hotter than ever in the new video, and has taken a more grown up route with her new music.

You can now view the brilliant ‘Same Old Love’ video.  

Same old love is out now and Revival will be released on October 9th
