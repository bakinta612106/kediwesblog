Sir Anthony Ritossa Outlines Family Business Considerations for 2023

As the world continues to emerge from the pandemic, many family businesses are looking ahead to the next few years and wondering what they need to do to not just survive but thrive. For family businesses, this is especially important, as these businesses have often passed down through generations and are a source of pride for the entire family. Discussions at the recent Ritossa Global Family Office Investment Summit focused on best practices for families as we enter 2023.

A few takeaways that a family business needs to understand as 2023 approaches include –

Family Members Need to be Adaptable

One of the most important things for any business to understand is that the world is constantly changing, and to stay ahead of the curve, you need to be adaptable. This is especially true for family businesses, as they may have set ways of doing things that are no longer relevant or effective. To ensure that your family business can be the best it can be in 2023, you need to be willing to adapt to the changing landscape.

 Technology is Key

In today’s day and age, technology is more important than ever before. Family businesses need to make sure that they are up-to-date on all the latest technology trends. This means having a strong online presence, as well as making use of things like data analytics, machine learning and artificial intelligence. By staying on top of the latest technology trends, you will be able position your family business for success. 

Customer Focus is Critical

No matter what type of business you are in, it is always important to focus on your customers, and make sure that you are providing them with what they want and need. This means constantly asking for feedback and making changes based on their feedback. By putting customers first, you will be able to keep them happy and ensure continued success for your family business. 

Family businesses play an important role in both the local economy and in society. They need to be adaptable, make use of technology, and focus on their customers. By understanding these three things, family businesses will be well-positioned for success in 2023 and future years to come.
