Ellen DeGeneres and Portia De Rossi Celebrate 10th Anniversary With Touching Wedding Footage Watc

Ten years ago, Ellen DeGeneres and Portia De Rossi tied the knot in a loving ceremony, and now, they’re ringing in their milestone anniversary by sharing touching footage from their wedding day on Instagram.

“Ten years ago today. This was such a special day for us,” Ellen captioned a video of herself getting a first look at Portia in her dress on their wedding on Thursday, Aug. 16. Portia followed up with the same video with the caption, “#10YearAnniversary #MarriageEquality.” Watch the sweet clip below!

The couple also acknowledged their anniversary last year. At the time, Ellen posted a black and white snap from their wedding day with the caption, “@PortiadeRossi and I got married 9 years ago today.”

Portia additionally posted a cute pic of the two of them to her Instagram. She wrote, “We’ve been together so long that not only do we look like kids in this picture, but it was taken with a camera! Happy 9th, Thing.” So in love!

The couple has been plagued by divorce rumors over the past few years, but it appears a split isn’t on their radar, as they continue to gush over one another in interviews time and time again. “Portia understands me completely,” Ellen recently shared. “In our vows, she recited a quote, ‘It is good to be loved. It is profound to be understood’ — and that to me is everything. She supports me and makes me happy.”

And Portia feels the same about Ellen! “We’re just really good friends and we talk about everything, and we never ever, ever lie to each other, like, not even the tiniest lies,” she once told Entertainment Tonight. “And she’s the only person I want to hang out with. Like, she is my best friend and I just want to be with her all the time.” She added, “She’s just so awesome! Who wouldn’t want to be with Ellen all the time?” So true!
