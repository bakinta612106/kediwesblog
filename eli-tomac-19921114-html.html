Eli Tomac is an American professional motorcyclist who competes in the AMA Supercross and Motocross Championships. Tomac started his professional career in 2010. He made his 450cc debut halfway through the 2013 Supercross calendar and joined the full-time class the following year. He won his first outdoor 450cc event at Spring Creek National 2014 in Millville, Minnesota, USA. His first 450cc Supercross win was in 2015 at Chase Field in Phoenix, Arizona. Eli has been part of the Kawasaki factory racing team since 2016 season
