Jennifer Lopez Says Shed Like To Have Babies With Alex Rodriguez

Jennifer Lopez and Alex Rodriguez are on their way to the aisle and it turns out that she even wants more kids with her fiancé. While on The Hoda Show, the triple threat told the host that she would love to expand her family.

At some point during the interview, Hoda did not hesitate to just ask: ‘Do you want more children?’

Just as straight to the point, Jennifer replied: ‘Yeah!’

As fans know, J.Lo. is already a mother of two – 11 year old twins Max and Emme – fathered by her former husband, Marc Anthony.

At the same time, Alex is also the father of Natasha, 14, and Ella, 11, with his former wife, Cynthia Scurtis.

The pair’s kids seem to get along with each other really well and the parents like to share a lot of posts featuring their happy blended family.

But what if they had a kid together as well?

Jennifer raved over her man during the interview, saying: ‘I honestly think God brought us together because we are so similar, but we both make each other raise our game as people. He has been really successful on his own, and I have had my success as well.’
