About Cherry Creek Schools District

Cherry Creek School District 5 is located in Colorado’s western Arapahoe County. Cherry Creek Public Schools is another name for it. Scott Siegfried is the director. It includes middle, elementary, as well as high schools.

It was founded in 1950 and is based in Greenwood Village. It serves Cherry Hills Village and Greenwood Village as well as most of Centennial, Foxfield and Glendale. It also serves parts of Englewood and Aurora as well as other suburban Denver areas.

Cherry Creek School District serves 54,000 students and more than 300,000. It covers 108 square miles, which is spread across eight municipalities.

Step by Step Guide

Follow these simple steps to access your My Cherry Creek account

  • Go to the My Cherry Creek login official site at my.cherrycreekschools.org.
  • In the input box, please enter your Username, Email Address, and Password.
  • To access your My Cherry Creek Account, please click the “LOGIN” button.

Forgot your Cherry Creek Password?

Follow these simple steps to recover your My Cherry Creek password.

  • Go to the My Cherry Creek login official site at my.cherrycreekschools.org
  • Click on the link “FORGOT PASSWORD?” as shown in the screenshot.
  • In the empty box, please enter your email address.
  • Please click the “NEXT” button and follow the instructions to reset your Forgot Password.
