Anthony Davis born March 11, 1993, is an American professional basketball player for the Los Angeles Lakers of the NBA. He plays the power forward and center positions. Davis is an eight-time NBA All-Star and has been named to four All-NBA First Teams and four NBA All-Defensive Teams. In his first season with the Lakers, he won an NBA championship in 2020. In 2021, he was named to the NBA 75th Anniversary Team. Davis played one season of college basketball for the University of Kentucky where he was a first-team All-American and the Consensus National Player of the Year. He also won the USBWA National Freshman of the Year, NABC Defensive Player of the Year, and the Pete Newell Big Man Award. As of now, Anthony Davis is currently married to Marlen Davis.
