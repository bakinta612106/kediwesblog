Kristin Cavallaris Twitter Page Shows us Shes Grown Up a Lot

We’ve been watching Kristin Cavallari for more than a decade since she made her debut as the villainous high school student in MTV’s Laguna Beach. She’s been manipulative, hateful, vindictive and completely unrelatable; but she’s managed to somehow turn her reputation around. Gone is the young and immature girl we all got to know on reality television and in her place is a girl who is a bit more mature, a bit more welcoming and a bit more likable. She’s now the mother of two small boys and the wife of a famous NFL quarterback. She’s grown up a lot since she was stealing boyfriends and making enemies on reality television, and now she’s more apt to work a business deal, post a selfie at the gym or post a photo of the healthy meals she’s cooking for her family on one of her social media accounts. That’s why we’re all starting to love Kristin Cavallari, especially now that she’s using her Twitter account for more relatable and enjoyable tweets. If you haven’t taken the time to check out Kristin Cavallari’s Twitter feed, do it. She’s got a lot more to offer than you might previously have thought. Take a look.
