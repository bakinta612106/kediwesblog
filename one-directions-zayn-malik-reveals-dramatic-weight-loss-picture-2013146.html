One Direction's Zayn Malik Reveals Weight Loss: Picture

It seems the pressure of being an international pop star is taking a toll on One Direction singer Zayn Malik. The 20-year-old Brit appears to have recently dropped a significant amount of weight from his already-slim frame, and an insider tells Us Weekly it's likely due to the stress of his busy schedule.

"He's lost a lot of weight," the source says. "He started to eat healthy a while ago and working out to get in shape as he had never trained before. He wanted some definition."

(Indeed, Malik recently tweeted about enjoying a nutritious snack. "Think I'm gonna have some weetabix with a banana now," he wrote on June 2.)

"He's always been the slimmest of the band members, naturally," the insider adds, "but I think he's running around a lot, getting stressed, and not putting health first."
